# 📚 AI Company Knowledge Assistant (RAG)

> An enterprise AI knowledge assistant powered by **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** that enables employees and customers to ask natural language questions and receive accurate answers based on approved company documents.

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Organizations store valuable knowledge across PDFs, Word documents, policies, manuals, SOPs, FAQs, contracts, and internal documentation.

Finding accurate information is often slow because employees must search through multiple documents manually.

This leads to:

- Lost productivity
- Repetitive support requests
- Inconsistent answers
- Knowledge silos
- Poor customer experience

---

# 💡 Solution

This AI-powered Knowledge Assistant uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to search approved company documents before generating AI responses.

Instead of relying solely on the language model, the assistant retrieves relevant information from the company's knowledge base, ensuring accurate and context-aware answers.
